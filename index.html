Low Groupon App Retention

Groupon gets opened for purchases, not browsing. How do we create daily-use habits that drive 3-4x weekly opens?

→

Why This Matters

  • Mobile traffic dominates — desktop extension (Genie) misses the majority of users
  • Cashback incentive gives Groupon control over initial offer quality vs relying on merchant coupon inventory
  • Once app installed, re-education and activation can happen over time
  • Creates a persistent presence in users' shopping flow outside the Groupon app

Why This Matters

  • High-frequency apps (Fetch, Temu, Shopkick) decouple "reason to open" from "intent to buy"
  • Groupon only gets opened for purchases — cashback creates a non-transactional return trigger
  • Visible balance creates sunk-cost motivation and earning streaks
  • Target: 3-4 opens per week without purchase intent

Why This Matters

  • Cashback is a natural account-creation trigger — unlike rewards gating, it feels like getting something, not losing access
  • App install follows naturally: "track your cashback balance" is a compelling reason to download
  • Dual benefit: solves Problem 2 (conversion) and feeds Problem 1 (retention via Groupon Bucks balance)
