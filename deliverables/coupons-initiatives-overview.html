Initiative 4

Cashback & Conversion Programme

Performance-based cashback + voucher upsell
40-50 MD eng

Two-part model: (1) Negotiate elevated CPA rates on select merchants and pass a share back to users as cashback. (2) Let users convert cashback balance into Groupon vouchers at a premium ($10 cash -> $12-15 voucher). Transforms one-off coupon seekers into logged-in, high-value Groupon customers.

Honest economics: Gross commission nearly doubles but ~42% goes back to users as payout. Net uplift is +11% (+$220K). Downside risk (-$500K) is larger than upside. The strategic value is in registration uplift (+1M) and SEO defense, not direct margin.

Strategic value beyond margin
Reduces SEO reliance by shifting users into logged-in, CRM-reachable cohorts. Builds first-party data (purchase intent, brand preferences). Defends against AI search disruption by adding value layers competitors lack.
