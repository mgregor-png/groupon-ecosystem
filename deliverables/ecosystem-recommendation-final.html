02
The Concept

By delivering coupons content through a Safari extension within the Groupon app, we extend Groupon's reach across all our customers' shopping experiences.

🛒

Thousands of brands

Nike, Amazon, Target, Sephora - everywhere they shop

🛒

Millions of checkouts

Codes + cashback applied automatically at purchase

📱

One app

No separate download - ships inside the Groupon app

03
How It Works

Three steps to everywhere

1

Ship inside the app

A Safari Web Extension is bundled with the Groupon iOS app. Users enable it once in Settings.

2

Show up at checkout

User shops at Nike. Groupon pops up with codes + cashback - automatically saving them money.

3

Bucks pull them back

Bucks earned everywhere, redeemed on local deals. Every off-platform save drives an app open.

Rather than the Genie extension which only works on desktop, this works on mobile - where our customers shop. 61% of our traffic is mobile.

Acquisition is supercharged: 8-10M app users already have the app. No separate download.
Personalisation data: Off-Groupon shopping data enhances in-app recommendations.

The Clever Bit

Groupon Bucks - The Connective Tissue

A visible, growing balance that turns off-platform shopping into in-app engagement.

Shop anywhere → Groupon finds savings → Earn Bucks → Check balance (habit) → Spend on local deals → Earn more Bucks
Every coupon lookup = exposure to a core deal.
Every core deal visit = exposure to coupons.
The wallet connects both worlds.
Earned this month $14.60 Expires in 67 days

Users check their balance even when they're not shopping. That's the daily open trigger.

06
The Full Picture

Why we need all three

Rather than a standalone extension with just coupons content, the real value is the ecosystem.

Distribution

Safari Extension = Reach

Extends Groupon into every shopping session. The distribution channel that puts Groupon on every checkout page.

Value

Cashback / Bucks = Value

Creates value without relying on merchant-issued codes. Opens the upsell into Groupon Bucks - the pull-back mechanism.

Retention

Coupons in App = Personalisation

Follow brands, get alerts, build a profile. Data from off-Groupon shopping enhances in-app recommendations.

Standalone coupons extension = limited. All three together = ecosystem.
