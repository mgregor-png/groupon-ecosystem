02 - The Big Idea

By delivering coupons content through a Safari extension within the Groupon app, we can extend Groupon's reach to deliver value for our customers across all their shopping experiences.

This captures users off-platform and through Groupon Bucks brings them back into the app.

03 - How It Works

How It Works

1

A Safari extension is added to the Groupon app

2

When a user downloads the app and enables the extension, Groupon will pop up when they shop at leading retailers on their phone

3

Coupons content is strengthened by cashback offers which upsell into Groupon Bucks, leading the user back to Groupon

Rather than the Genie extension which only works on desktop, this works on mobile - where our customers shop!

Supercharged acquisition: Leverages the existing app base - 8-10M MAU, no separate download needed.
Personalisation data: Integrating data from users' off-Groupon shopping experience into their Groupon profile enhances opportunity for personalisation.

05 - The Full Picture

Safari Extension + Cashback/Bucks + Coupons in the App

Why We Need All Three

Rather than a standalone extension with just coupons content, the real value is based on the extension + cashback/Bucks + coupons account/app integration.

Distribution

Safari Extension

Reach - extends Groupon into every shopping session on mobile Safari. The distribution channel.

Value

Cashback / Groupon Bucks

Value creation - gives us a way of creating valuable content for the user without being reliant on codes issued by merchants. Most importantly, cashback opens the doorway to an upsell into Groupon Bucks - pulling users back to the core app.

Retention

Coupons in the App

Personalisation - integration into the account opens the way for more messaging. Allow customers to follow their favourite brands, get alerts when coupons/cashback offers are available.
