Groupon Safari Extension

Solving Groupon app retention through the open web. A 7-beat walkthrough of the concept, the user flow, and the open questions for this review.

The problem

Groupon app retention is the bottleneck

Users come to Groupon's web Coupons properties, redeem a code, and leave. Web traffic doesn't compound into Groupon app sessions. The relationship ends at the single redemption.

The concept

The Safari extension is the bridge

An iOS Safari extension brings Groupon's coupons and cashback into every web shopping moment. Every redemption earns Groupon Bucks - a wallet that only lives inside the Groupon app.

The loop

Earn on the web, spend in the app, earn more

Web shopping converts into Bucks. Bucks convert into Groupon-marketplace bookings. Bookings earn the next round of Bucks. The flywheel turns and the user is now an app user, not a one-shot coupon redeemer.
