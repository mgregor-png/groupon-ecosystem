## What It Is
A discovery initiative exploring how cashback can plug into the Groupon ecosystem — using Coupons organic traffic as the acquisition funnel.

## The Proposed Flow
1. **Registration capture** — Use cashback offers on Coupons pages to convert anonymous organic visitors into registered users
2. **Groupon Bucks upsell** — Convert cashback users into Groupon shoppers via Groupon Bucks incentive
3. **App download incentive** — Use cashback tracking as a hook to drive app installs

## POC Proposal
- **Effort:** 32 MD
- **Two key metrics to validate:**
  1. How many user registrations do cashback offers drive from Coupons traffic?
  2. How many of those users convert to Groupon Bucks/shoppers?
