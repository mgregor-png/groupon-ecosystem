Local Deals Promo Copy — Dollar Incentive

Same "dollars not percentages" strategy, tied to local experiences. Transition-ready for eventual cashback.

Strategy: Use fixed dollar amounts ($10, $15, $20) instead of "25% off" to cut through the coupon noise. Tie the incentive to local experiences (spas, dining, activities) so it feels like a gift to try something, not just another discount. Language is chosen to bridge toward cashback later — "money back", "credit", "on us".
