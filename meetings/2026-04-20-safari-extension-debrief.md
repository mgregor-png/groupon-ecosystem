## What to measure on each competitor (reverse engineering)

- Signup flow (including any bonus - e.g. Capital One $45)
- Onboarding emails (cadence, content, hooks)
- Cashback / rewards mechanics (points, soft currency, breakage handling)
- Traffic sources / acquisition (SimilarWeb + AI on earnings calls / investor docs)
- Sign-up trajectory, sessions, retention proxies
- LTV estimation (from public filings where possible)
- Loyalty mechanics: how they make users come back weekly
- Cashback breakage factor (Dusan's signal: ~50% never claim - bake into model)
- Is it profitable? Some competitors run cashback even at $0 commission to build habit.
